Chicken Piccata


Briny capers and a tangy, fresh lemon sauce top tender pieces of breaded chicken in this easy, restaurant-quality recipe for chicken piccata. The sauce is so addicting, you'll want to drink it! Serve with pasta.

Ingredients:

  • Chicken Breast
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Flour
  • Olive Oil
  • Butter
  • Dry White Wine
  • Capers
  • Chicken Stock
  • Lemon Juice
  • Lemon Zest
  • Fresh Parsley

Steps:

  1. Season and coat chicken in flour.
  2. Heat oil and cook chicken.
  3. Combine butter, wine, and capers in skillet.
  4. Add chicken stock, lemon juice, and lemon zest. Stir.
  5. Return chicken breast to skillet and cook in sauce.
  6. Remove from heat, serve over pasta.
